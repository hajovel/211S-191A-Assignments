about me

critical mapmaking

As someone who finds it very important to continue learning about critical social issues, I hope that throughout this course and as a critical mapmaker, I can amplify marginalized voices whilst respecting spaces and communities I am not in by not speaking over or misrepresenting them. As a critical mapmaker, I want to create compelling maps visually but also maps that are representative of my goal in always being a good ally to others. As someone who has little previous experience in making a map, I am looking forward to learning and using maps for social justice and take this knowledge to criticize the maps outside this course and woven into everyday life, now aware of the injustice so many reflect.
